# Resumax

**Build a sharp, ATS-friendly resume that's tailored to the jobs you actually want.**

🌐 **Live at [resumax.io](https://resumax.io)**

Resumax turns your experience into a clean, professionally typeset resume. Pick a
template, fill in your details (or import an existing YAML config), and export a
polished PDF rendered with real LaTeX typesetting — no fiddling with formatting,
no fighting with Word. Your data stays in your browser until you ask for a render.

## What it does

- **Beautiful templates** — LaTeX-typeset resumes that look hand-crafted, not
  template-y.
- **Tailor to the job** — shape your resume around the roles you're targeting.
- **Grading report** — every check shows points, evidence, and a concrete fix so
  you know exactly how to make your resume stronger. No dark patterns, no fake
  "ATS-optimized" badges.
- **Import / export** — start from a saved resume config and get a clean PDF back.
- **Privacy-first** — anonymous by default; your data lives in the browser unless
  a render is in flight.

## The story

I built Resumax because [Resumake](https://resumake.io) stopped serving.

Resumake helped me land a job — it made building a resume that got noticed
genuinely easy, right when I needed it. When it went down, I wanted to give
others the same head start it gave me. Resumax is that: a free resume builder,
built to help you get the interview.
